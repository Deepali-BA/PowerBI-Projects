
# Amazon Sales Dashboard – Business Insight Analysis

**Business Analyst Portfolio Project | Power BI | Data Analysis**

This project analyzes global Amazon sales data to identify **revenue trends, category performance, market contribution, geographic sales distribution, and shipping preferences**.

The dashboard converts raw transactional data into **actionable business insights** that help stakeholders understand sales performance and make data-driven decisions.

---

# Executive Summary

The dashboard analysis of Amazon sales data indicates strong global revenue performance with **total sales of $12.64M generated from 178K units sold**.

The business achieved **$1.47M in total profit while incurring $1.35M in shipping costs**.

Technology products contribute the largest share of revenue, while the **APAC region leads global market sales**.

---

# Key Performance Indicators

| KPI                 | Value   |
| ------------------- | ------- |
| Total Sales         | $12.64M |
| Total Units Sold    | 178K    |
| Total Profit        | $1.47M  |
| Total Shipping Cost | $1.35M  |

These KPIs provide a high-level overview of overall business performance.

---

# Category-wise Sales Insights

Technology is the **top-performing category**, contributing approximately **37.5% of total sales ($4.74M)**.

Other category contributions include:

* **Furniture:** $4.11M (32.5%)
* **Office Supplies:** $3.79M (30%)

**Insight:**
Technology products generate the largest share of revenue, indicating strong customer demand in this category.

---

# Market-wise Sales Insights

The **APAC region leads global sales with $3.59M (28.36%)**.

Other major markets include:

* **EU:** $2.94M (23.24%)
* **US:** $2.3M (18.17%)

LATAM and EMEA collectively contribute over **23% of total sales**, highlighting growing demand in emerging markets.

**Insight:**
The Asia-Pacific region represents the strongest performing global market.

---

# City-level Sales Performance

Top performing cities by revenue include:

* New York City
* Los Angeles
* Manila

**Insight:**
Major metropolitan cities contribute significantly to overall sales performance due to higher purchasing power and stronger market demand.

---

# Shipping Mode Analysis

Sales distribution by shipping mode shows:

* **Standard Class** generates the highest sales volume
* **Second Class** and **First Class** contribute moderate sales
* **Same Day shipping** contributes the lowest share

**Insight:**
Customers generally prefer **cost-effective delivery options rather than premium fast shipping services**.

---

# Country-level Sales Insights

The **United States leads global country-level sales**.

Other high-performing countries include:

* Australia
* France
* China
* Germany
* Mexico
* India

**Insight:**
Developed markets dominate revenue generation while emerging economies show potential for future growth.

---

# Key Business Insights

* Technology category is the **primary revenue driver**
* APAC region contributes the **largest share of global sales**
* New York City is the **top-performing city by revenue**
* Standard shipping mode dominates **customer preference**
* The United States leads **country-level sales performance**

---

# Strategic Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategic actions are recommended:

**Focus on High-performing Product Categories**

Expand technology product offerings to leverage strong customer demand.

**Strengthen Presence in APAC Market**

Increase marketing investment and supply chain support in the APAC region.

**Optimize Shipping Costs**

Shipping costs are close to total profit and should be optimized to improve margins.

**Target High-performing Cities**

Focus promotional campaigns in cities generating high revenue.

**Expand Emerging Markets**

Develop targeted marketing strategies in LATAM and EMEA regions.
