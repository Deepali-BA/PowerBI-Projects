# Amazon Sales Performance Dashboard

**Business Analyst Case Study | Power BI | Data Analysis**

This project analyzes global Amazon sales data to identify **revenue trends, top-performing markets, product category performance, and shipping preferences**. The objective is to convert raw sales data into **actionable business insights** that can support strategic decision-making.

---

# Project Objective

E-commerce companies generate large volumes of transactional data across **multiple regions, product categories, and shipping methods**. Without structured analysis, it becomes difficult to understand:

* Which **products drive the most revenue**
* Which **markets contribute the highest sales**
* Which **cities and countries perform best**
* What **shipping methods customers prefer**

This project aims to answer these questions through **interactive Power BI dashboards and data analysis**.

---

# Dataset Overview

The dataset contains global Amazon sales transaction data including:

| Column        | Description                      |
| ------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Order ID      | Unique identifier for each order |
| Order Date    | Date when order was placed       |
| Ship Mode     | Delivery method used             |
| Category      | Product category                 |
| City          | Delivery city                    |
| Country       | Customer country                 |
| Market        | Regional market                  |
| Sales         | Revenue generated                |
| Profit        | Profit earned                    |
| Shipping Cost | Delivery cost                    |
| Quantity      | Number of products sold          |

# Dashboard Insights

## Category-wise Sales Contribution

Technology is the highest revenue-generating category **($4.74M)** followed by Furniture **($4.11M)** and Office Supplies **($3.79M)**.

**Insight:**
Technology products drive the largest share of revenue.

---

## Market-wise Sales Performance

APAC contributes the highest sales **($3.59M)** followed by EU **($2.94M)** and US **($2.3M)**.

**Insight:**
Asia-Pacific region is the strongest performing market.

---

## Top Cities by Sales

Top performing cities include:

* New York City
* Los Angeles
* Manila

**Insight:**
Major metropolitan cities contribute significantly to global revenue.

---

## Shipping Mode Analysis

Standard Class shipping accounts for the highest sales volume.

**Insight:**
Customers prefer **cost-effective delivery options** over premium fast shipping.

---

## Country-level Sales Performance

The **United States leads global sales**, followed by Australia and France.

**Insight:**
Developed markets dominate revenue generation.

---

# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

**Focus on Technology Products**

* Expand technology product offerings.

**Strengthen APAC Market**

* Increase marketing and logistics support in APAC.

**Optimize Shipping Costs**

* Shipping costs are close to total profit and require optimization.

**Target High-performing Cities**

* Focus promotions in top-performing cities.
